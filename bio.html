BIOGRAPHY

INTRODUCTION: I was born in the family with the reigned cult of creativity. With my sister, a talented and original artist (Anna Makarevich), we loved to read and write stories since our childhood.

In our family fairy tales in verse and prose were written by grandparents, mom and dad. And our children compose fairy stories as well: my grown-up son, younger daughter and nephew.

BIOGRAPHY: Myroslava Makarevych - children's writer, journalist. She has published three fairy tale books: "The Magic Van" (2006); "The Constellation of Love" (2012). "A Tale of Chestnut and Rowan" (2014). The participant of the Prague book fair 2015. Ukraine participated in this event for the first time!

She has the over 25 years of journalism experience including BBC and Reuters, publishing and editorial background as Editor-in-Chief at HFS, Edipresse Ukraine, Sanoma Media Ukraine publishing houses.

A journalist profession gave to Myroslava the opportunities to visit many countries, open many doors closed to ordinary tourists, meet and interview the iconic personalities of nowadays. And all this is a source of inspiration to create new and new fairy tale stories.

Miroslava was born on May, 1972 in New York (USA).
